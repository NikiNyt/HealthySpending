Documentation for Store Owners

Why Join HealthySpending?

If you have your own shop where you sell healthy nutrition for reasonable prices, you can offer to add your business to our database after analysis from our team. We will send notifications about different sales and other options to users of our project.

Getting Started

Step 1: Submit Your Business

  • Fill out the business submission form with your store details
  • Provide information about healthy products you offer
  • Include pricing information and any special offers

Step 2: Verification Process

  • Our team will review your submission within 5-7 business days
  • We may contact you for additional information
  • Upon approval, your store will be added to our database

Best Practices

  • Offer special discounts for HealthySpending users
  • Keep your inventory information up to date
  • Respond to customer feedback promptly
  • Highlight seasonal healthy products
üí° Pro Tip: Stores with special offers for project users get featured more prominently in search results!

Documentation for City Explorers

What is a City Explorer?

You can visit different shops, restaurants, and cafes, send data about items you can buy there and prices for them with a mark on the map in businesses you consider acceptable for our goal. After analysis from our team, we will add these places to our project and every user will be able to find them with ease.

How to Contribute

Step 1: Find a Location

  • Visit local shops, restaurants, and cafes
  • Look for places offering healthy food options at reasonable prices
  • Take note of the exact address and business hours

Step 2: Document Your Findings

  • Take photos of healthy food items (optional but helpful)
  • Record prices of key healthy products
  • Note any special features (organic, local, bulk options, etc.)

Step 3: Submit Your Discovery

  • Use our submission form to add the location
  • Mark the exact location on the map
  • Include all relevant details and pricing information

Rating System & Rewards

Active city explorers earn points for each verified submission! Your contributions help you climb the leaderboard, and top contributors receive special recognition and rewards.

üèÜ Earn More Points:
  • First to discover a new location: +50 points
  • Complete location with photos: +30 points
  • Regular updates to existing locations: +10 points

Documentation for Nutritionists

Join Our Analytics Team

If you are a professional nutritionist and can prove that, you can contribute to our project by joining the analytics team which grades businesses by their healthy nutrition goods. Also, you can create articles which will be shown in a special tab on our project's website.

Verification Process

Required Credentials:

  • Professional certification or license in nutrition/dietetics
  • Proof of education (degree in nutrition, dietetics, or related field)
  • Professional references or portfolio of work

Your Role as a Nutritionist

Business Analysis:

  • Review and grade submitted businesses based on their healthy food offerings
  • Provide detailed assessments of product quality
  • Verify health claims made by businesses

Content Creation:

  • Write educational articles about nutrition
  • Create guides for healthy eating on a budget
  • Answer community questions in the forum
  • Review and recommend specific products

Impact & Recognition

As a verified nutritionist, your feedback carries more weight in our rating system. You'll also be featured as a trusted expert on our platform, helping thousands of people make better food choices.

üìù Article Topics We Need:
  • Reading nutrition labels effectively
  • Meal planning on a budget
  • Seasonal eating guides
  • Understanding macronutrients
